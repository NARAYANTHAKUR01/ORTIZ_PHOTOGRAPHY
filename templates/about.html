MY STORY

Since 2015, the Brandi Ortiz Studio has taken a curious, narrative-driven lens to showcase the myriad of people who call New York City home.

From the debut author who just released a bestseller to the aspiring model creating their first portfolio, Brandi Ortiz helps you tell your story through light and airy photos.

Brandi Ortiz has been featured in local, national, and international publications and won awards for editorial art direction. The Brandi Ortiz Studio is synonymous with personal expression.
